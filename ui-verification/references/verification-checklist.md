# Verification Checklist

## Visual Quality

- Is the visual direction obvious and product-specific?
- Does typography create hierarchy?
- Does spacing clarify groups?
- Are surfaces used intentionally?

## States

- Are empty, loading, error, success, disabled, focus, and selection states covered where relevant?
- Are destructive actions and validation states explicit?

## Accessibility

- Is contrast addressed?
- Is text scaling or equivalent adaptation considered?
- Is keyboard or touch navigation clear?
- Is reduced motion addressed?

## Platform Fit

- Does navigation match the target platform?
- Do layout, density, and interaction patterns fit the device and input model?
- Is intentional divergence documented for multi-platform work?
