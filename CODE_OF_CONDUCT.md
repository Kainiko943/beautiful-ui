# Code of Conduct

This project follows the Contributor Covenant spirit: be respectful, constructive, and focused on helping people build better interfaces.

## Expected behavior

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Be kind when reviewing design work.
- Critique ideas and examples, not people.
- Keep discussions practical and evidence-oriented.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Dismissive feedback without actionable detail.
- Spam, trolling, or unrelated promotion.

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or block contributors who make the project worse for the community.
